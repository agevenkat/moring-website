Why insurance agentic AI stalls

Three reasons the pilot dies in Legal review.

Carriers aren't slow to adopt because the technology fails. They're slow because the substrate underneath the technology has nothing to show the examiner.

Pain 1 · Audit

The exam request lands. You can't answer.

The market conduct examiner asks for the reasoning chain behind a denial. Your team has logs of API calls and a screenshot. That isn't evidence.

Pain 2 · Model risk

Your model inventory has a black box in it.

SR 11-7-style governance demands versioned models, validated inputs, and documented performance bounds. Vendor SDKs and ad-hoc prompts don't qualify.

Pain 3 · Cost

Finance can't allocate AI spend to a line of business.

Without per-policy or per-claim attribution, AI spend hits a corporate cost center. Actuaries can't price for it. CFO won't fund the next deployment.
