Solution 02 · AI for Customer Support

Support agents that actually finish the job.

Moring builds multi-agent customer support for the tickets that take real work: warranty claims, payment disputes, regulated complaints, billing investigations. The agents pull the documents, check the policy, and draft the resolution. A human reviewer signs off on anything that lands in a regulator-watched queue. Handle time comes down, and QA coverage gets to every conversation instead of a sample.

Book the workshop → How we approach support

01 · How we think about it

Human in the loop is a feature. Not an apology.

Customer experience is the wrong place to chase full automation. A regulated complaint touches CFPB. A warranty denial touches a CSAT score. The substrate is built so the agent does the document-heavy work and the human signs the consequential call. The reviewer's queue is shorter and the work in it is more interesting. Coverage goes from a 2% sample to every conversation.

02 · Where it applies

Six places to start.

Each one is scoped against a real escalated queue. The agents do the document gather and policy check. The human signs the resolution.

WARRANTY90 days

Warranty claims.

An intake agent reads the customer narrative, the warranty terms, the receipt, and the photos. A policy agent checks coverage. A drafter writes the approval or denial. The reviewer signs. The denial letter is the same letter the regulator will read.

DISPUTES90 days

Disputes and chargebacks.

A dispute agent pulls the transaction, the merchant pattern, the customer history, and Reg E or Reg Z timers. Drafts the provisional credit decision and the merchant response. Stops the clock on Reg E exposure.

AUTH60 days

Authentication and identity.

Step-up auth flows that don't punish the legitimate customer. Voice biometrics, knowledge factors, and document re-verification orchestrated by a single agent that knows the risk score and the customer's history.

DEFLECTION90 days

Tiered deflection and triage.

Tier 1 to 3. The agent finishes the easy ticket, routes the medium one to the right team with the documents pre-pulled, and escalates the hard one with a summary the senior tier can act on in one read.

VOICE120 days

Voice and contact center.

Real-time copilot for the contact center agent. Reads the conversation, suggests the next step, drafts the call summary, files the disposition. Every minute the human saves goes back into the conversation.

COMPLAINTS90 days

Regulated complaint capture.

For CFPB, FCA, APRA regulated channels. Captures the complaint correctly the first time, tags it against the right product and disclosure, and starts the response clock. A human signs every regulated response.

03 · How we ship this

AI-DLC in customer support.

Same four pillars as every other engagement. Specific agent names, specific compliance gates.

// pillar 01 · agents

Three agents per workflow.

An intake parser reads the ticket and pulls the attachments. A policy checker reads the terms, the warranty doc, the regulation. A resolution drafter writes the customer-facing response. Each one has one job.
