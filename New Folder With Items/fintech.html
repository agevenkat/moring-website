Why bank agentic AI stalls

Three reasons the rollout dies in the second line.

Banks aren't slow because the technology fails. They're slow because the substrate underneath the technology can't produce the evidence the second-line risk team is paid to demand.

Pain 1 · Model risk

Your model inventory says "GenAI" and nothing else.

SR 11-7 expects versioned models, validated inputs, monitored outputs. Ad-hoc prompts in a developer's IDE are not in your inventory.

Pain 2 · Data exfil

One pasted secret is a 90-day disclosure.

Engineers paste API keys, customer records, and prod data into prompts. Without a perimeter hook, the disclosure clock starts the moment the request leaves your VPC.

Pain 3 · Allocation

AI spend hits a corporate cost center.

Without per-team, per-LOB attribution, Finance can't charge AI to the line of business that consumed it. The next budget cycle stalls. The platform team takes the hit.
